How It Works

The SuraDAS AI Pin seamlessly integrates into your daily life with intuitive voice interactions and audio feedback.

01

Attach it anywhere

The AI Pin attaches magnetically to your clothing, making it easy to wear with any traditional or modern Indian attire.

02

Activate with voice

A simple voice command in Hindi, English, or any of 10+ Indian languages brings the AI Pin to life.

03

Ask naturally
